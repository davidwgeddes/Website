I'm excited to announce that my personal website is now live! After years of having a simple static site, I decided it was time for an upgrade to something more modern and maintainable.

## What's New

The site has been completely rebuilt using **Hugo**, a fast and flexible static site generator, combined with the clean and minimalist **PaperMod theme**. This gives the site a fresh, professional look while keeping things simple and performant.

## The Setup

For those interested in the technical side:
- **Static Site Generator**: Hugo
- **Theme**: PaperMod (lightweight, no dependencies)
- **Hosting**: GitHub Pages
- **Content**: Written in Markdown for easy updates

The entire site is version-controlled on GitHub, making it easy to track changes and maintain the codebase over time.

## What You'll Find

Right now, the site features:
- **About/Home** - A brief intro about what I do
- **Projects** - Showcasing my app development projects including Bible Reader, Racquet Stringer, and HushSMS
- **Blog** - Where I'll be sharing thoughts and updates (like this post!)

## In Development

This is just the beginning! The site is actively being developed and improved. Some things on the horizon:

- More detailed project documentation
- Regular blog posts about development topics
- Enhanced project demos and links
- Additional customization and refinement

If you notice anything that could be improved or have suggestions, feel free to reach out. I'm always open to feedback!

Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you find the projects interesting.
