A comprehensive job management app built specifically for professional racquet stringers to track, organize, and manage their restringing jobs efficiently.

## Features

- **Job Tracking** - Log all restringing jobs with customer details
- **Multiple Sports** - Support for Tennis, Badminton, and Squash racquets
- **Customer Management** - Maintain customer profiles and preferences
- **Service History** - Track stringing history for each racquet
- **Pricing Calculator** - Built-in pricing and quote generation
- **Invoice Generation** - Professional invoices for customers
- **Statistics & Analytics** - Track productivity and revenue
- **Offline Mode** - Work without internet connection

## Tech Stack

- Mobile-first design (iOS/Android)
- Local database for job storage
- Cloud sync for backups
